Questions

FAQ

Can I try before I buy?+

Absolutely. We believe in the East Blue experience. Every new member is entitled to a 7-day Elite Trial Pass. This gives you full, unrestricted access to all our studio locations, classes, and our AI Neural Coach so you can feel the results for yourself before committing.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. We value flexibility. At East Blue Gym, we believe in results, not contracts. You can cancel your monthly membership at any time directly through your performance dashboard. For annual plans, we offer a 30-day "Results Guaranteed" window where you can cancel for a full refund if you're not satisfied.

How many locations can I access?+

Your membership is a global passport. Starter members enjoy access to their primary home studio. Pro and Elite members gain "Global Passport" status, granting them unlimited access to over 8,200+ partner studios and elite East Blue locations across 50 countries.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?+

Of course. Your fitness journey is dynamic. You can instantly upgrade your plan at any time through the dashboard to unlock more features. Downgrades take effect at the beginning of your next billing cycle, ensuring you always have the right level of support for your current goals.

Is there a family or corporate plan?+

We offer specialized "Team Elite" packages for families and corporate groups of 5 or more. These include shared performance tracking, dedicated account management, and significant group discounts. Contact our partnership team for a custom-tailored quote.
